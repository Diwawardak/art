SHOWREEL 2020

NIGHTS OF THE LAKE/ LES NUITS DES BASSINS

21-22nd September 2019 - Arras, France

An interactive, sound-responsive piece created using the software Resolume for 'Les Nuits des Bassins' festival. The sculptures were made out of found objects and had microphones placed inside which required the instruments to be played or spoken into in order to generate the animation on the screens.
